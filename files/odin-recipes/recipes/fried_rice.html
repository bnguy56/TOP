Garlic Fried Rice

My Garlic Fried Rice recipe!

Here are the ingredients you will need to create this recipe.

  • 3 Cups of cooked rice.
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic (the more the merrier!)
  • 3-4 eggs
  • 1 table spoon: msg, chicken powder, soy sauce, sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon: sugar,
  • Optional: choice of protein

Step by step instructions

  1. Grab a pan, and put in oil on lowest heat
  2. Chop garlic to a fine dice
  3. Add garlic to pan, and saute for a minute or two
  4. Add in cooked, but dry or cold rice to pan
  5. Add in seasonings
  6. Optionally add in cooked protein of choice here
  7. Stir fry for a few minutes until sizzling
  8. Add in soy sauce and sesame oil, and toss
  9. Plate and enjoy!
