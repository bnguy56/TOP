Spaghetti

The finished product my spaghetti and meat balls recipe!

Here are the ingredients you will need to create this recipe.

  • 1 pound of dry angel hair pasta.
  • 28 ounce jar of pasta sauce.
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic (the more the merrier!)
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 table spoon - oregano, parsley, basil
  • 1 pound of meat balls
  • 1 pound of sausage
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Step by step instructions

  1. Fill a pot half way full, and add generous amount of salt (1tbsp), bring to boil
  2. Grab a pot, and put in oil on lowest heat
  3. Chop to a fine dice: onion and garlic
  4. Add garlic to pot, and saute for a minute or two
  5. Add in the onions, and leave on low heat to soften
  6. Add dry pasta to boiling water, and submerge noodles, boil for 10 minutes
